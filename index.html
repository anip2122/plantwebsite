FAQ

How do I choose the right plant for my home?

Consider the light in your space, the amount of care you can provide, and the size of the plant when fully grown.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for most plants, provided they are in their original condition.

Can I order plants online?

Yes, you can browse and order directly from our website. We offer delivery and pickup options for your convenience.
